Sometimes someone comes into your life who seems to offer you everything you want, everything you need.

If you’re prepared, and lucky, the timing aligns so you can invest fully.

More often, the timing is wrong.

You might still be healing, slow to trust.

The circumstances of your life might pull your attention, leaving you less available than you’d hoped.

Sometimes, being present, the most important thing, is the hardest.

And sometimes, you can’t give yourself.
